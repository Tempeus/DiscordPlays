# DiscordPlays
While I'm streaming Valorant, people in my discord chat can take control of my game.

## Commands:
Shoot: Shoots my gun for 1 second. <br />
Entry: Holds W for 10 seconds <br />
VC: Hold down my voice chat button for 10 seconds <br />
Crouch: Holds down the crouch button for 5 seoncds <br />
Jump: Make me jump <br />
Drop: Drop my gun <br />
Pistol: Switch to my pistol <br />
Knife: Switch to my knife <br /> 
Reload: Make me reload <br />
Random_ability: Make me use a random ability (C, Q or E) <br />
America: Make me hold down the shoot button for 10 seconds <br />
Slippery_Hands: Make me drop my gun every 10 seconds for 10 times <br />
Reload Paranoia: Make me reload a gun every 5 seconds for 20 times <br />
