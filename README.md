# DiscordPlays
While I'm streaming Valorant, people in my discord chat can take control of my game.

## Commands:
Shoot: Shoots my gun for 1 second.
Entry: Holds W for 10 seconds
VC: Hold down my voice chat button for 10 seconds
Crouch: Holds down the crouch button for 5 seoncds
Jump: Make me jump
Drop: Drop my gun
Pistol: Switch to my pistol
Knife: Switch to my knife
Reload: Make me reload
Random_ability: Make me use a random ability (C, Q or E)
America: Make me hold down the shoot button for 10 seconds
Slippery_Hands: Make me drop my gun every 10 seconds for 10 times
Reload Paranoia: Make me reload a gun every 5 seconds for 20 times
